About

The site of the magazine is aimed at providing quick and accurate information to users by gathering instantaneous news from news agencies and reputable sites. In choosing these sites, full respect for the laws, regulations and interests of Canada has been considered. The Magazine site (www.magazine.com) was launched in June 2017 and now provides news and information to 24 hour readers. This site provides users with the latest news, pictures, reports and analyzes on separate pages including the home page and pages of Canada and the world, culture and art, science and technology, sports, economics and commerce.
