Advertise with us

Buy an advert on any of our web pages and let your business be seen by thousands of our visitors. We offer ads as Text Link and Banner Ads. A text link is a title or phrase (maximum 30 characters) that when clicked, our visitor is redirected to your website/blog.

The choice of your title or phrase determines the number of users that click your link. It is very important to choose your words carefully. Banner Ads are images or animated images in jpg, jpeg, bmp or gif format. If you do not have a banner, use can use the same space with text. Your banner must be hosted in your website however we can host your banner for a small fee. Business owners and affiliates may take this opportunity to attract traffic to their website, blog or affiliate link. Your ad is permanent for the time period purchased, that is, your ad will not be in rotation with other ads. Please read our Terms of Service carefully before you purchase any ad.
